# Perficient Education & Tuition Benefits

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** March 1, 2024  
**Policy Owner:** VP of Learning & Development  
**Applies To:** All full-time Perficient employees with 6+ months of tenure

---

## 1. Tuition Reimbursement Program

### 1.1 Eligible Programs
Perficient reimburses tuition costs for degree programs that are relevant to the employee's current role or a reasonable future career path at Perficient:
- Bachelor's degrees
- Master's degrees (MBA, MS in Computer Science, Data Science, etc.)
- Professional doctorates (relevant fields only)
- Associate degrees and certificate programs from accredited institutions

### 1.2 Reimbursement Amounts
| Program Level | Annual Maximum | Lifetime Maximum |
|---------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Undergraduate | $7,500/year | $30,000 |
| Graduate (Master's) | $10,000/year | $40,000 |
| Doctorate | $12,000/year | $50,000 |
| Certificate Programs | $5,000/year | $15,000 |

### 1.3 Eligible Expenses
- Tuition and mandatory fees
- Required textbooks and course materials
- Lab fees and technology fees
- Parking permits (up to $200/semester)
- NOT eligible: Room and board, meal plans, transportation, application fees, late fees

### 1.4 Requirements
- Must maintain a GPA of 3.0 or higher (B average)
- Must be enrolled at an accredited institution
- Must receive pre-approval from manager AND L&D team before each semester
- Must submit grade report and receipts within 60 days of course completion
- Grade of C or below: No reimbursement for that course
- Withdrawal: No reimbursement unless documented medical/emergency reason

### 1.5 Service Commitment
- Reimbursement over $5,000/year requires a 2-year service commitment
- If employee voluntarily leaves within commitment period, prorated repayment is required
- Involuntary termination (not for cause): Repayment obligation waived

## 2. Professional Certification Program

### 2.1 Sponsored Certifications
Perficient covers 100% of exam fees and study materials for the following certifications:
- **Cloud:** AWS Solutions Architect, Azure Administrator/Architect, GCP Professional
- **Development:** Salesforce (all tracks), Adobe Experience Manager, Sitecore
- **Data:** Databricks, Snowflake, Tableau, Power BI
- **Project Management:** PMP, CSM, SAFe Agilist, PRINCE2
- **Security:** CISSP, CompTIA Security+, CCSP
- **Other:** TOGAF, ITIL, Six Sigma (Green/Black Belt)

### 2.2 Certification Benefits
- **Exam Fee:** Covered in full (first attempt); 50% covered for retakes (max 2 retakes)
- **Study Materials:** Up to $500 per certification (books, practice exams, video courses)
- **Study Time:** Up to 40 hours of paid study time per certification (scheduled with manager)
- **Certification Bonus:** $500 one-time bonus upon passing (deposited in next paycheck)
- **Renewal Fees:** Company pays renewal/maintenance fees for active certifications relevant to current role

### 2.3 Process
1. Submit certification request in Workday Learning (include justification and target date)
2. Manager approves (within 5 business days)
3. L&D team registers employee for exam and orders materials
4. Employee completes certification
5. Submit proof of completion to L&D for bonus processing

## 3. Conference & Event Attendance

### 3.1 Annual Conference Budget
- **Individual Budget:** $3,000/year per employee for conferences and events
- **Covers:** Registration fees, travel, hotel, meals (per Perficient travel policy)
- **Approval:** Manager approval required; events over $2,000 require Director approval

### 3.2 Speaking Engagements
Employees presenting at conferences receive:
- Full conference costs covered (does not count toward $3,000 individual budget)
- Additional preparation PTO day
- $300 speaker honorarium
- Company promotional support (social media, blog post)

### 3.3 Internal Tech Talks
- **Frequency:** Bi-weekly internal tech talks (30-60 minutes)
- **Speakers:** Any employee can volunteer to present
- **Recognition:** Tech Talk speakers earn 2x Virgin Pulse wellness points

## 5. Internal Mobility & Career Development

### 5.1 Internal Job Marketplace
- All open positions posted internally for 5 business days before external candidates considered
- Employees can apply after 12 months in current role (6 months with manager support)
- Internal transfers receive same onboarding support as external hires

### 5.2 Mentorship Program
- **Formal Program:** 6-month structured mentoring (matched by L&D team based on career goals)
- **Executive Mentoring:** Senior leaders mentor high-potential employees (nominated by VP)
- **Reverse Mentoring:** Junior employees mentor executives on emerging tech topics
- **Time Commitment:** 2 hours/month minimum

### 5.3 Leadership Development
- **Emerging Leaders Program:** 12-week cohort program for senior individual contributors transitioning to management
- **Management Accelerator:** Quarterly workshops for new managers (first 2 years)
- **Executive Development:** Annual external executive coaching (Director+ level)

## 6. Student Loan Assistance

### 6.1 Monthly Contribution
- **Amount:** $100/month contributed directly to employee's student loan servicer
- **Eligibility:** Full-time employees with qualified education loans (federal or private)
- **Lifetime Maximum:** $10,000 per employee
- **Start Date:** Benefits begin the first month following enrollment

### 6.2 Enrollment Process
1. Submit loan documentation (account number, servicer name, outstanding balance) via Workday
2. HR verifies loan qualifies
3. Payments begin the following month via direct transfer to servicer
4. Employee receives confirmation in Workday each month

### 6.3 Tax Treatment
- Company contributions are currently taxable income to the employee
- Contribution appears on W-2 as imputed income
- Employee may deduct student loan interest paid (per IRS rules)

## 7. Innovation & Research Time

### 7.1 Innovation Friday
- **Allocation:** One Friday per month designated for self-directed learning and innovation
- **Activities:** Personal projects, hackathons, open-source contributions, research, prototyping
- **Requirement:** Document learning/output in Confluence; present quarterly findings to team
- **Manager Override:** May be paused during critical project phases (max 2 consecutive months)

### 7.2 Patent & Publication Support
- **Patent Bonus:** $2,000 for filed patent + $3,000 additional if patent is granted
- **Publication Bonus:** $500 for published technical articles (blog, journal, whitepaper)
- **Legal Support:** Company IP attorney assists with patent applications at no cost to employee
