# Perficient Retirement & Financial Benefits

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** February 15, 2024  
**Policy Owner:** VP of Total Rewards & Finance  
**Applies To:** All full-time Perficient employees (unless otherwise specified)

---

## 1. 401(k) Retirement Savings Plan

### 1.1 Plan Overview
Perficient's 401(k) plan is administered by Fidelity Investments and is designed to help employees build long-term retirement savings with tax advantages and company matching contributions.

### 1.2 Eligibility & Enrollment
- **Eligibility:** All full-time employees from Day 1 of employment
- **Auto-Enrollment:** New hires are automatically enrolled at 6% contribution rate (can opt out or change within 30 days)
- **Auto-Escalation:** Contributions automatically increase 1% per year on January 1 (up to 15% maximum, unless employee opts out)

### 1.3 Contribution Limits (2024)
- **Employee Contribution:** Up to $23,000 per year (pre-tax or Roth)
- **Catch-Up Contribution (age 50+):** Additional $7,500 (total $30,500)
- **Combined Limit (employee + employer):** $69,000

### 1.4 Company Match
- **Match Formula:** 50% of employee contributions up to 6% of eligible compensation
- **Effective Maximum Match:** 3% of salary
- **Match Timing:** Calculated and deposited each pay period
- **True-Up:** Year-end true-up ensures employees receive full match regardless of contribution timing

### 1.5 Vesting Schedule
| Years of Service | Vesting % |
|-----------------|-----------|
| Less than 1 year | 0% |
| 1 year | 33% |
| 2 years | 66% |
| 3+ years | 100% |

Employee contributions are always 100% vested immediately.

### 1.6 Investment Options
- **Target-Date Funds:** Vanguard Target Retirement series (2025-2065)
- **Index Funds:** S&P 500, Total Stock Market, International, Bond Index
- **Active Funds:** 15+ actively managed options across asset classes
- **Self-Directed Brokerage:** Fidelity BrokerageLink for individual stock/ETF trading
- **Stable Value Fund:** Capital preservation option (3.2% current rate)
- **ESG Options:** 2 ESG-focused fund choices

### 1.7 Loans & Withdrawals
- **Loans:** Up to 50% of vested balance (maximum $50,000); repayment via payroll deduction over 1-5 years (15 years for primary residence)
- **Hardship Withdrawals:** Available for qualifying expenses (medical, education, primary residence, funeral, eviction prevention); subject to taxes and 10% penalty if under 59½
- **In-Service Distributions:** Available at age 59½

## 2. Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP)

### 2.1 Plan Details
- **Stock:** Perficient, Inc. (NASDAQ: PRFT)
- **Discount:** 15% off the lower of the stock price at the beginning or end of the offering period
- **Contribution:** 1% to 15% of base salary (after-tax payroll deductions)
- **Purchase Periods:** Semi-annual (January 1 - June 30 and July 1 - December 31)
- **Annual Limit:** $25,000 in stock (at fair market value per IRS rules)

### 2.2 Eligibility
- Full-time employees with 6+ months of service
- Must not own more than 5% of Perficient stock
- Enrollment windows: December 1-15 and June 1-15

### 2.3 Tax Treatment
- **Qualifying Disposition:** Shares held 2+ years from offering date AND 1+ year from purchase — taxed at long-term capital gains rate on discount portion
- **Disqualifying Disposition:** Discount taxed as ordinary income; gain above purchase price taxed at capital gains rate

## 3. Financial Planning Services

### 3.1 Fidelity Financial Wellness
- **One-on-One Advising:** Free 30-minute consultations with a Fidelity advisor (unlimited)
- **Online Tools:** Retirement planning calculators, income projections, asset allocation guidance
- **Webinars:** Monthly financial literacy webinars (investing basics, tax planning, estate planning, Social Security)

### 3.2 Financial Coaching (Ayco by Goldman Sachs)
- **Eligibility:** Director level and above (or employees with household income $150,000+)
- **Services:** Comprehensive financial planning, tax optimization, equity compensation strategies, estate planning
- **Cost:** Company-subsidized (employee pays $50/quarter; company covers $200/quarter)
- **Frequency:** Quarterly sessions with dedicated financial coach

### 3.3 Student Loan Assistance
- **Monthly Contribution:** Perficient contributes $100/month toward qualified student loan payments
- **Eligibility:** Full-time employees with federal or private student loans
- **Maximum Lifetime Benefit:** $10,000 per employee
- **Enrollment:** Submit loan documentation through Workday; payments begin the following month
- **Continuation:** Benefit continues until loan is paid off or lifetime max is reached

## 4. Deferred Compensation Plan

### 4.1 Eligibility
Available to employees at the VP level and above, or those with total compensation exceeding $250,000.

### 4.2 Plan Details
- **Contribution:** Defer up to 50% of base salary and up to 100% of bonus
- **No IRS Contribution Limits:** Unlike 401(k), no annual cap
- **Investment Options:** Same fund lineup as 401(k) plus additional alternatives
- **Distribution:** Lump sum or installments upon separation, retirement, or scheduled in-service date
- **Unfunded Plan:** Subject to company's general creditors (Section 409A compliance)

## 5. Equity Compensation

### 5.1 Restricted Stock Units (RSUs)
- **Eligibility:** Senior Manager level and above; individual contributors with exceptional performance
- **Vesting:** Typically 4-year vesting (25% per year on anniversary date)
- **Tax Treatment:** Taxed as ordinary income at vesting; withholding at supplemental rate (22% federal)

### 5.2 Performance Stock Units (PSUs)
- **Eligibility:** Director level and above
- **Performance Period:** 3-year cliff vesting based on company revenue growth and TSR targets
- **Payout Range:** 0% to 200% of target grant
- **Measurement:** Reviewed by Compensation Committee of the Board

## 6. Referral Bonus Program

### 6.1 Standard Referral Bonus
- **Amount:** $3,000 for standard professional hires
- **Payment:** 50% at hire date; 50% after referred employee completes 90 days
- **Eligibility:** All non-recruiting employees can refer

### 6.2 Premium Referral Bonus
- **Amount:** $5,000 for hard-to-fill roles (as designated by Talent Acquisition)
- **Amount:** $7,500 for executive-level referrals (Director+)
- **Payment:** Same 50/50 split structure

### 6.3 Rules
- Referred candidate must list referrer's name on application
- Referrer cannot be the hiring manager or in the direct interview chain
- No limit on number of referrals per year
- Bonus is taxable income

## 8. Emergency Financial Assistance

### 8.1 Perficient Cares Fund
- **Purpose:** Short-term financial assistance for employees facing unexpected hardship (natural disaster, medical emergency, death in family)
- **Grant Amount:** Up to $5,000 per event (non-repayable)
- **Application:** Submit request to HR confidentially; reviewed within 5 business days
- **Funding:** Employee donations (company matches 2:1) and corporate allocation
- **Eligibility:** Employees with 6+ months of tenure who have exhausted PTO and other benefits
