# Perficient Health & Wellness Benefits

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** April 1, 2024  
**Policy Owner:** VP of Total Rewards  
**Applies To:** All full-time Perficient employees; part-time employees working 20+ hours/week eligible for medical only

---

## 1. Medical Insurance Plans

Perficient partners with UnitedHealthcare to offer three comprehensive medical plan options:

### 1.1 Preferred Provider Organization (PPO)
- **Monthly Premium:** $185 (employee-only) / $520 (employee + family)
- **Deductible:** $500 individual / $1,000 family
- **Out-of-Pocket Maximum:** $4,000 individual / $8,000 family
- **Primary Care Visit:** $25 copay
- **Specialist Visit:** $50 copay
- **Emergency Room:** $250 copay (waived if admitted)
- **Prescription Drugs:** $10 generic / $35 preferred brand / $60 non-preferred
- **Network:** Access to 900,000+ providers nationwide
- **Out-of-Network:** Covered at 60% after deductible

### 1.2 High-Deductible Health Plan (HDHP)
- **Monthly Premium:** $95 (employee-only) / $310 (employee + family)
- **Deductible:** $1,500 individual / $3,000 family
- **Out-of-Pocket Maximum:** $6,000 individual / $12,000 family
- **All Services:** Subject to deductible, then 80/20 coinsurance
- **Preventive Care:** 100% covered (no deductible)
- **HSA-Eligible:** Yes — Perficient contributes $750/year (individual) or $1,500/year (family)
- **Network:** Same UnitedHealthcare network as PPO

### 1.3 Health Maintenance Organization (HMO)
- **Monthly Premium:** $75 (employee-only) / $240 (employee + family)
- **Deductible:** $0
- **Out-of-Pocket Maximum:** $3,000 individual / $6,000 family
- **Primary Care Visit:** $20 copay
- **Specialist Visit:** $40 copay (referral required from PCP)
- **Emergency Room:** $200 copay
- **Network:** In-network only (except emergencies)
- **Availability:** Select metro areas (Dallas, St. Louis, Minneapolis, Atlanta)

## 2. Health Savings Account (HSA) & Flexible Spending Account (FSA)

### 2.1 Health Savings Account (HSA)
Available only to HDHP enrollees:
- **2024 Contribution Limits:** $4,150 individual / $8,300 family (includes employer contribution)
- **Perficient Contribution:** $750 individual / $1,500 family (deposited quarterly)
- **Investment Threshold:** Balances over $1,000 can be invested in mutual funds
- **Rollover:** Unlimited — funds never expire
- **Triple Tax Advantage:** Pre-tax contributions, tax-free growth, tax-free qualified withdrawals

### 2.2 Healthcare FSA
Available to PPO and HMO enrollees:
- **2024 Contribution Limit:** $3,200
- **Carryover:** Up to $640 into the following year
- **Eligible Expenses:** Medical, dental, vision copays, prescriptions, medical devices
- **Dependent Care FSA:** Separate election up to $5,000/year for childcare/eldercare expenses

---

## 3. Dental Insurance

### 3.1 Delta Dental PPO
- **Monthly Premium:** $28 (employee-only) / $82 (family)
- **Preventive (cleanings, x-rays):** 100% covered, no deductible
- **Basic (fillings, extractions):** 80% after $50 deductible
- **Major (crowns, bridges, implants):** 50% after $50 deductible
- **Annual Maximum:** $2,000 per person
- **Orthodontia (adults and children):** 50% up to $2,000 lifetime maximum
- **Waiting Period:** None for preventive/basic; 12 months for major (waived for new hires enrolling within 30 days)

---

## 4. Vision Insurance

### 4.1 VSP Vision Care
- **Monthly Premium:** $12 (employee-only) / $34 (family)
- **Eye Exam:** $10 copay, once per year
- **Frames:** $200 allowance every 24 months
- **Lenses:** Covered in full (single, bifocal, progressive) annually
- **Contact Lenses:** $150 allowance per year (in lieu of frames/lenses)
- **Laser Vision Correction:** 15% discount at participating providers + $100 allowance

---

## 5. Mental Health & Behavioral Health

### 5.1 In-Network Therapy
- **Outpatient Therapy:** Same copay as specialist visits ($50 PPO / $40 HMO)
- **Providers:** Licensed therapists, psychologists, psychiatrists
- **Visit Limit:** None — unlimited sessions per year
- **Virtual Therapy:** Covered at same rate as in-person (Talkspace, BetterHelp in-network)

### 5.2 Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
- **Provider:** ComPsych GuidanceResources
- **Free Sessions:** 8 sessions per issue per year (employee + household members)
- **Services:** Counseling, substance abuse, grief support, relationship issues
- **Additional:** Financial counseling (3 sessions), legal consultation (30-minute sessions)
- **Access:** 24/7 hotline: 1-800-555-0199 | Online: guidanceresources.com (Org ID: PRFT2024)
- **Crisis Support:** Immediate telephonic crisis intervention available 24/7

### 5.3 Substance Abuse Treatment
- **Inpatient Rehabilitation:** Covered at 80% after deductible (up to 30 days/year)
- **Outpatient Programs:** Same as specialist copay
- **Medication-Assisted Treatment:** Covered under prescription drug benefit
- **Confidentiality:** All treatment records maintained separately from general medical

## 6. Wellness Program

### 6.1 Annual Wellness Reimbursement
- **Amount:** $600 per year per employee
- **Eligible Expenses:** Gym memberships, fitness classes, home exercise equipment, wellness apps, sports leagues, massage therapy
- **How to Claim:** Submit receipts through Workday Expenses within 90 days of purchase
- **Reimbursement Timeline:** Processed within 2 pay periods

### 6.2 Biometric Screening
- **Annual Screening:** Optional, offered on-site or at Quest Diagnostics locations
- **Incentive:** Complete screening = $200 HSA/HRA contribution
- **Tests Included:** Blood pressure, cholesterol, glucose, BMI, waist circumference
- **Results:** Confidential — shared only with employee and wellness coach (not employer)

### 6.3 Digital Wellness Tools
- **Headspace:** Premium subscription for meditation and sleep (free for all employees)
- **Noom:** Weight management program (subsidized — $50/quarter employee cost)
- **Virgin Pulse:** Activity tracking platform with rewards points redeemable for gift cards
- **Wellness Challenges:** Quarterly team challenges (steps, hydration, sleep) with prizes

### 6.4 Preventive Health
- **Annual Physical:** 100% covered (no copay, no deductible) for all plan members
- **Immunizations:** Covered in full (flu, COVID, hepatitis, travel vaccines)
- **Cancer Screenings:** Covered in full per USPSTF guidelines
- **Tobacco Cessation:** Free program including counseling + nicotine replacement therapy

---

## 7. Telehealth Services

### 7.1 Virtual Care (Teladoc)
- **Availability:** 24/7/365 for all enrolled employees and dependents
- **Cost:** $0 copay for general medical consultations
- **Services:** General health, allergies, cold/flu, skin conditions, prescription refills
- **Mental Health:** Virtual therapy sessions at standard specialist copay
- **Average Wait Time:** Under 10 minutes
- **App:** Available on iOS and Android

### 7.2 Virtual Specialist Consultations
- **Available Specialties:** Dermatology, cardiology, endocrinology, orthopedics
- **Cost:** Standard specialist copay ($50 PPO)
- **Referral:** Not required for virtual specialist visits

## 8. Disability & Income Protection

### 8.1 Short-Term Disability (STD)
- **Coverage:** 60% of base salary
- **Waiting Period:** 7 calendar days
- **Maximum Duration:** 26 weeks
- **Cost:** Company-paid
- **Eligibility:** After 90 days of employment

### 8.2 Long-Term Disability (LTD)
- **Coverage:** 60% of base salary (up to $15,000/month)
- **Waiting Period:** After STD exhaustion (26 weeks)
- **Maximum Duration:** To age 65 (or Social Security Normal Retirement Age)
- **Cost:** Company-paid
- **Definition of Disability:** Own occupation for first 24 months; any occupation thereafter

---

## 9. Life & Accidental Death Insurance

### 9.1 Basic Life Insurance (Company-Paid)
- **Coverage:** 2x annual base salary (maximum $500,000)
- **AD&D:** Equal to basic life amount
- **Cost:** Fully paid by Perficient
- **Effective:** First day of employment

### 9.2 Supplemental Life Insurance (Employee-Paid)
- **Employee:** 1x to 5x salary (up to $1,000,000); amounts over $200,000 require medical evidence
- **Spouse:** $10,000 to $250,000 in $10,000 increments
- **Child(ren):** $5,000 or $10,000 per child
- **Portability:** Can continue coverage within 31 days of termination

---

## 10. Enrollment & Eligibility

### 10.1 Eligibility
- **Full-Time Employees:** Eligible for all benefits on first day of employment
- **Part-Time (20+ hours):** Eligible for medical only (no dental, vision, or supplemental)
- **Dependents:** Legal spouse, domestic partner, children to age 26

### 10.2 Enrollment Windows
- **New Hire:** Must enroll within 30 calendar days of start date; if missed, must wait until Open Enrollment
- **Open Enrollment:** November 1-15 annually (January 1 effective date)
- **Qualifying Life Events:** 30-day window from event date (marriage, birth, adoption, divorce, loss of other coverage, relocation)

### 10.3 Benefits Contact
- **Benefits Team:** benefits@perficient.com
- **Phone:** 1-800-555-PRFT (option 3)
- **Office Hours:** Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM CT
- **Workday Self-Service:** View elections, download benefit cards, update dependents
