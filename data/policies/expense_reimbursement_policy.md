# Perficient Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** March 5, 2024  
**Policy Owner:** VP of Finance  
**Applies To:** All Perficient employees incurring business expenses

---

## 1. General Principles

Perficient reimburses employees for reasonable, necessary, and documented business expenses. This policy supplements the Travel & Expense Policy by covering non-travel business expenses.

---

## 2. Reimbursable Expenses

### 2.1 Technology and Equipment
- Mobile phone: $100/month reimbursement for employees required to be on-call or use personal devices for business
- Internet service (remote employees): $75/month
- Home office setup (new hires): One-time $500 stipend
- Monitor, keyboard, peripherals: Pre-approved by manager, up to $500/year
- Software subscriptions not available through IT: Requires IT approval first

### 2.2 Professional Memberships
- Professional associations relevant to role (IEEE, ACM, PMI): Fully reimbursable
- Maximum 2 memberships per year without additional approval
- Student memberships for supported degree programs: Covered

### 2.3 Client Entertainment
- Client meals: Per Travel & Expense Policy limits
- Client gifts: Maximum $150/recipient/year (see Code of Conduct)
- Team building with clients: Pre-approved by Account Manager

### 2.4 Office Supplies
- Remote employees: Up to $50/quarter without pre-approval
- Office-based employees: Use office supply closet or submit facilities request
- Specialized supplies (ergonomic accessories, whiteboards): Manager approval

### 2.5 Professional Attire
- Company-branded apparel for events: Provided by Marketing
- Business attire for client presentations: Not reimbursable (considered personal)
- Safety equipment or uniforms (if role-required): Fully covered

---

## 3. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are NOT eligible for reimbursement:
- Personal electronics (tablets, personal phones, smartwatches)
- Home internet speed upgrades beyond basic business needs
- Furniture beyond the initial $500 home office stipend
- Co-working space memberships (unless pre-approved for employees without home office space)
- Commuting costs (home to office)
- Personal professional services (resume writing, headshots, coaching)
- Fines, penalties, or late fees
- Personal subscriptions (Netflix, Spotify, personal news)
- Alcohol purchased independently (not part of client/team dinner)
- Spouse/partner expenses at any business event

---

## 4. Approval Workflow

### 4.1 Pre-Approval Requirements
Pre-approval is required for:
- Any single expense over $250
- Technology purchases over $100
- Conference registration
- Client entertainment over $100
- Any recurring subscription commitment

### 4.2 Approval Authority

| Amount | Approver |
|--------|----------|
| Under $100 | No pre-approval (manager reviews on report) |
| $100 - $500 | Direct manager |
| $500 - $2,000 | Director |
| $2,000 - $10,000 | VP |
| Over $10,000 | CFO |

---

## 5. Expense Report Submission

### 5.1 Platform
All expense reports submitted through Concur Expense. Access via:
- Web: concur.perficient.com
- Mobile app: SAP Concur (iOS/Android)
- Email forwarding: receipts@concur.perficient.com (auto-attached to profile)

### 5.2 Submission Requirements
Each expense must include:
- Date of purchase
- Vendor/merchant name
- Business purpose (brief justification)
- Itemized receipt (for expenses over $25)
- Attendees (for meals with others)
- Project/cost center code (for billable expenses)

### 5.3 Deadlines
- Monthly recurring expenses: Submit by the 5th of the following month
- One-time expenses: Submit within 14 days of incurrence
- End of year: All Q4 expenses must be submitted by January 10
- Late submissions (>60 days): May not be reimbursed without VP exception

### 5.4 Missing Receipts
- Contact vendor for duplicate receipt first
- If unavailable: Complete Missing Receipt Affidavit in Concur
- Maximum 3 missing receipt affidavits per quarter
- Amounts over $75 without receipt require manager written justification

## 6. Corporate Credit Card

### 6.1 Eligibility
Corporate credit cards (Chase Visa) are issued to:
- Employees who travel 2+ times per quarter
- Employees with recurring reimbursable expenses over $200/month
- Managers with team entertainment responsibilities
- Request through Finance (cc-request@perficient.com)

### 6.2 Usage Rules
- Business expenses only (personal use is a policy violation)
- Reconcile statements monthly in Concur (due by 15th of following month)
- Report lost/stolen cards to Chase immediately: 1-800-XXX-XXXX and Finance
- Card limit: $10,000 (higher limits available with VP approval)

### 6.3 Delinquent Reconciliation
- 30 days past due: Email reminder from Finance
- 60 days past due: Manager notified, card temporarily suspended
- 90 days past due: Card cancelled, balance deducted from paycheck (with notice)

---

## 7. Billable vs. Non-Billable Expenses

### 7.1 Client-Billable Expenses
Expenses that can be billed to clients (per SOW):
- Travel to client site
- Client-approved tools or licenses
- Materials for client deliverables
- Overtime meals during critical client milestones

Must be tagged with project code in Concur. Subject to client approval before billing.

### 7.2 Internal (Non-Billable) Expenses
- Internal training and development
- Company meetings and offsites
- Internal tool subscriptions
- Recruiting expenses
- General office supplies

---

## 8. Reimbursement Processing

### 8.1 Timeline
- Manager approval: Within 5 business days of submission
- Finance processing: Within 5 business days of manager approval
- Payment: Direct deposit, next regular pay cycle after processing
- Total typical turnaround: 10-15 business days from submission

### 8.2 Payment Method
- All reimbursements via direct deposit to payroll bank account
- International employees: Local currency equivalent
- No cash advances (except for international travel with Finance pre-approval)

### 8.3 Disputes
- Contact finance-expenses@perficient.com for rejected or partial reimbursements
- Provide additional documentation or justification
- Escalation to VP of Finance if unresolved within 10 business days
