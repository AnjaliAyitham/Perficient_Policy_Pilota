# Perficient Workplace Safety and Health Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** January 20, 2024  
**Policy Owner:** VP of Facilities & Operations  
**Applies To:** All Perficient employees, contractors, and visitors at Perficient facilities

---

## 1. Commitment to Safety

Perficient is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment for all employees, contractors, and visitors. We comply with all applicable OSHA regulations and strive to exceed minimum safety requirements.

---

## 2. Office Safety

### 2.1 General Office Safety
- Keep walkways, exits, and hallways clear of obstructions
- Report damaged furniture, equipment, or fixtures to Facilities immediately
- Clean up spills promptly and place wet floor signs
- Use handrails on stairs; do not run in offices
- Keep file cabinet drawers closed when not in use
- Ensure proper cable management to prevent trip hazards
- Maximum occupancy limits posted and enforced for all rooms

### 2.2 Ergonomics
- Workstation assessments available upon request through Facilities
- Adjust chairs, monitors, and desks to ergonomic standards
- Take regular breaks from prolonged sitting (recommended: stand/stretch every 60 minutes)
- Report pain or discomfort related to workstation setup to manager and HR
- Standing desks available upon request (subject to availability)

### 2.3 Electrical Safety
- Do not overload electrical outlets or power strips
- Report frayed cords, sparking outlets, or overheating equipment immediately
- Do not use personal space heaters (fire hazard)
- Unplug equipment when not in use for extended periods
- Only use UL-listed electrical equipment

## 3. Emergency Procedures

### 3.1 Fire Emergency
- Upon hearing fire alarm or discovering fire:
  1. Alert others in immediate area
  2. Pull nearest fire alarm if not already activated
  3. Evacuate using nearest exit (do NOT use elevators)
  4. Proceed to designated assembly point
  5. Report to Floor Warden for headcount
  6. Do not re-enter building until authorized by Fire Department
- Fire extinguisher use: Only if trained and safe to do so (PASS method: Pull, Aim, Squeeze, Sweep)

### 3.2 Severe Weather
- **Tornado Warning:** Move to interior rooms on lowest floor; away from windows
- **Earthquake:** Drop, Cover, Hold On; evacuate after shaking stops if building is damaged
- **Severe Storm:** Remain indoors; avoid windows; await all-clear from Facilities
- Weather alerts distributed via building PA system and Everbridge notification

### 3.3 Medical Emergency
1. Call 911 immediately for life-threatening emergencies
2. Notify front desk/reception (ext. 0)
3. Stay with the person until help arrives
4. AED locations: Reception area, break rooms on each floor, gym
5. First aid kits: Every floor, kitchen areas, and reception

### 3.4 Active Threat
- **RUN:** If safe to do so, evacuate immediately via nearest exit
- **HIDE:** If evacuation is not possible, find a secure room, lock/barricade door, silence phone
- **FIGHT:** As absolute last resort, use available objects to disrupt the threat
- Call 911 when safe. Do not approach the threat.

### 3.5 Evacuation
- Know your primary and alternate evacuation routes (posted on each floor)
- Assist persons with disabilities (evacuation buddies assigned)
- Assembly points designated for each building (minimum 500 feet from building)
- Floor Wardens conduct sweep and headcount
- Do not return to building until officially cleared

## 4. Visitor Safety

### 4.1 Visitor Registration
- All visitors must sign in at reception and receive visitor badge
- Visitors must be escorted by a Perficient employee at all times
- Visitor badges must be returned upon departure
- Large groups (10+) require advance notification to Facilities

### 4.2 Contractor Safety
- Contractors performing physical work must provide proof of insurance
- Hot work (welding, cutting) requires hot work permit from Facilities
- Contractors must follow all building safety rules
- Perficient contact must be on-site during contractor work

---

## 5. Incident Reporting

### 5.1 What to Report
Report ALL workplace incidents, including:
- Injuries (however minor) — cuts, falls, strains
- Near-misses (situations that could have caused injury)
- Property damage
- Environmental hazards (chemical spills, water leaks)
- Unsafe conditions or behaviors
- Violence, threats, or aggressive behavior

### 5.2 How to Report
- Immediate danger: Call 911, then Facilities ext. 5000
- Non-emergency incidents: Submit report through Workday Safety module within 24 hours
- Anonymous safety concerns: safety@perficient.com or Safety Hotline ext. 5555
- Workers' compensation injuries: Notify HR within 24 hours

### 5.3 Investigation
All reported incidents are investigated by:
- Facilities team (for physical/environmental hazards)
- HR (for workplace violence or behavioral concerns)
- Security (for criminal activity)
- Root cause identified and corrective actions documented within 5 business days

## 6. Health and Wellness

### 6.1 Indoor Air Quality
- HVAC systems maintained per ASHRAE standards
- Annual air quality testing in all offices
- No smoking within 25 feet of building entrances
- Scent-free zones designated for sensitive employees

### 6.2 Kitchen and Break Room Safety
- Refrigerators cleaned weekly (unclaimed items discarded Fridays)
- Microwave and kitchen appliances inspected monthly
- No personal cooking appliances (toaster ovens, hot plates) at desks
- Allergen awareness: Label personal food; clean up immediately after use

### 6.3 Fitness Center (where available)
- Waiver required before use
- Proper athletic attire and closed-toe shoes required
- Wipe down equipment after use
- No personal training without facility approval
- Report equipment malfunctions immediately

---

## 7. Environmental Health

### 7.1 Hazardous Materials
- Safety Data Sheets (SDS) maintained for all chemicals on-site
- Cleaning supplies stored in designated, ventilated areas
- Proper PPE required for handling any chemical products
- Spill kits located near chemical storage areas

### 7.2 Waste Management
- Recycling bins provided (paper, plastic, metal, electronics)
- E-waste disposed through approved vendor (quarterly collection)
- Confidential document shredding bins on every floor
- Batteries and toner cartridges: Designated collection points

---

## 8. Training

### 8.1 Required Safety Training
- **All employees:** Fire safety and evacuation (annual, during Fire Prevention Week)
- **All employees:** Active threat awareness (annual)
- **Floor Wardens:** Enhanced emergency response (semi-annual)
- **First responders:** First aid and CPR/AED certification (maintained current)
- **New hires:** Safety orientation during onboarding week

### 8.2 Drills
- Fire evacuation drill: Semi-annual (one announced, one unannounced)
- Severe weather drill: Annual (spring)
- Floor Warden exercises: Quarterly

---

## 9. Remote Worker Safety

### 9.1 Home Office Safety
Remote employees are responsible for maintaining a safe home workspace:
- Adequate lighting to prevent eye strain
- Smoke detectors and fire extinguisher accessible
- Clear pathways (no trip hazards from cables or clutter)
- Proper electrical setup (no overloaded circuits)
- Ergonomic furniture and equipment

### 9.2 Workers' Compensation Coverage
Work-related injuries in the home workspace during working hours are covered:
- Report immediately to manager and HR
- Document the incident (photos, description, witnesses if applicable)
- Seek medical attention as needed
- Complete incident report in Workday within 24 hours

---

## 10. Compliance

### 10.1 Regulatory Compliance
Perficient complies with:
- OSHA General Industry Standards (29 CFR 1910)
- State-specific workplace safety regulations
- Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) accessibility requirements
- Local fire codes and building safety requirements

### 10.2 Safety Committee
- Cross-functional Safety Committee meets monthly
- Reviews incident reports, trends, and corrective actions
- Conducts quarterly workplace inspections
- Recommends policy updates and improvements
- Membership: Facilities, HR, employee representatives from each floor/location
