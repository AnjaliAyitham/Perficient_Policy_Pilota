# Perficient Family & Life Benefits

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** May 1, 2024  
**Policy Owner:** VP of People & Culture  
**Applies To:** All full-time Perficient employees with 6+ months of tenure (unless otherwise noted)

---

## 1. Parental Leave

### 1.1 Birth Parents
- **Duration:** 16 weeks of fully-paid leave
- **Extended Leave:** Additional 6 weeks at 60% pay (short-term disability)
- **Start Date:** Leave begins on date of birth or scheduled C-section
- **Flexibility:** May be taken continuously or in two blocks (minimum 4-week blocks with manager agreement)
- **Prenatal Appointments:** Up to 8 hours of paid time per month for prenatal medical visits during pregnancy

### 1.2 Non-Birth Parents (Partners, Adoptive, Foster)
- **Duration:** 12 weeks of fully-paid leave
- **Timing:** Must begin within 12 months of birth, adoption finalization, or foster placement
- **Flexibility:** May be taken in minimum 1-week increments
- **Bonding Leave:** Available to both same-sex and opposite-sex partners

### 1.3 Eligibility Requirements
- Completed 6 months of continuous employment
- In good standing (no active Performance Improvement Plan)
- Provide 30 days' advance notice when possible (emergencies excepted)
- Only one parental leave per 12-month rolling period per birth/placement event

### 1.4 Benefits During Parental Leave
- All insurance benefits continue (medical, dental, vision, life)
- Company continues full premium contributions
- PTO continues to accrue during leave
- 401(k) match continues based on contributions made in pay periods worked
- Equity vesting continues uninterrupted

## 2. Fertility & Family Planning Benefits

### 2.1 Fertility Treatment Coverage
- **Provider:** Progyny (integrated with UnitedHealthcare plans)
- **Smart Cycles:** 2 Smart Cycles included (each cycle covers a complete treatment — IVF, IUI, egg freezing, or other fertility treatment)
- **Lifetime Maximum:** $30,000 in fertility treatment coverage
- **Covered Services:** IVF, IUI, ICSI, egg/embryo freezing, genetic testing (PGT), donor egg/sperm, fertility medications
- **Fertility Medications:** Covered at 80% through specialty pharmacy (Optum)

### 2.2 Egg/Sperm Freezing
- **Elective Freezing:** 1 cycle covered for elective egg or sperm freezing
- **Storage:** First 2 years of storage covered by Perficient; employee cost thereafter ($500/year)
- **Eligibility:** All full-time employees regardless of current family status

### 2.3 Surrogacy Support
- **Agency Fees:** Up to $10,000 reimbursement for surrogacy agency fees
- **Legal Support:** Up to $3,000 for surrogacy legal costs
- **Parent Leave:** Full parental leave applies to intended parents via surrogacy

### 2.4 Fertility Navigation
- **Concierge Service:** Dedicated Progyny Patient Care Advocate assigned to each employee
- **Appointments:** Virtual or in-person consultations to discuss options and create a plan
- **Cost:** Free to employees enrolled in any Perficient medical plan

## 3. Adoption & Foster Care Assistance

### 3.1 Adoption Reimbursement
- **Amount:** Up to $10,000 per adoption event (domestic or international)
- **Eligible Expenses:** Agency fees, legal fees, court costs, home study, travel, immigration/visa fees, translation fees
- **Process:** Submit itemized receipts within 6 months of finalization
- **Limit:** 2 adoption events covered per employee lifetime

### 3.2 Foster Care Support
- **Foster Placement Stipend:** $500 per new foster placement (for initial supplies/setup)
- **Ongoing Allowance:** $100/month per foster child for clothing, activities, school supplies
- **Leave:** Full parental leave (12 weeks) applies for long-term foster placements (intended duration 6+ months)

### 3.3 Adoption Leave
- 12 weeks of fully-paid leave (same as non-birth parent leave)
- Begins upon legal placement of child
- Additional unpaid leave (up to 4 weeks) available with manager approval

## 4. Childcare Benefits

### 4.1 Dependent Care FSA
- **Contribution Limit:** $5,000/year (married filing jointly) or $2,500 (married filing separately)
- **Eligible Expenses:** Daycare, preschool, before/after school care, summer camp, in-home childcare
- **Child Age Limit:** Under age 13

### 4.2 Backup Childcare (Bright Horizons)
- **Days Available:** 15 days per year of subsidized backup childcare
- **Employee Cost:** $25/day (in-center) or $6/hour (in-home care)
- **Actual Value:** $100-200/day (Perficient covers the difference)
- **Use Cases:** Regular caregiver unavailable, school closures, snow days, travel emergencies
- **Booking:** Via Bright Horizons app or website (24-hour advance booking recommended; same-day available)

### 4.3 Childcare Subsidy
- **Amount:** $200/month per child (maximum $600/month for 3+ children)
- **Eligibility:** Employees with household income below $150,000/year
- **Age Range:** Children under age 6 enrolled in licensed childcare
- **Process:** Submit monthly daycare invoice in Workday; subsidy deposited in next paycheck

### 4.4 On-Site Childcare (Select Locations)
- Available at St. Louis headquarters (Tiny Scholars Learning Center)
- Priority enrollment for Perficient employees
- Discounted rate: 20% below market rate
- Ages: 6 weeks to 5 years
- Hours: 7:00 AM - 6:30 PM (aligned with business hours)

## 5. Elder Care & Caregiving

### 5.1 Elder Care Resources
- **Care.com Membership:** Premium membership (free) for finding in-home elder care providers
- **Geriatric Care Manager:** 3 free consultations per year with certified geriatric care manager
- **Support Group:** Monthly virtual caregiver support group (facilitated by EAP counselor)

### 5.2 Caregiver Leave
- **Duration:** Up to 4 weeks of paid caregiver leave per year
- **Eligible Reasons:** Caring for seriously ill parent, spouse, or child
- **Documentation:** Medical certification required from care recipient's physician
- **Intermittent Use:** May be taken in 1-day increments (with manager notification)

### 5.3 Elder Care FSA
- Eligible within the Dependent Care FSA ($5,000/year combined with childcare)
- Covers adult daycare, in-home aide, and other qualified expenses for tax dependents

## 6. Family Emergency & Bereavement

### 6.1 Bereavement Leave
| Relationship | Paid Days |
|-------------|-----------|
| Spouse/domestic partner | 10 days |
| Child | 10 days |
| Parent | 7 days |
| Sibling | 5 days |
| Grandparent, grandchild, in-law | 3 days |
| Aunt, uncle, cousin, close friend | 1 day |

- Additional unpaid leave (up to 2 weeks) available with manager approval
- Bereavement counseling through EAP (8 sessions)
- Pregnancy/infant loss: Same as child bereavement (10 days) regardless of gestational age

### 6.2 Family Emergency Leave
- **Duration:** Up to 5 paid days per year for family emergencies
- **Eligible Situations:** House fire, natural disaster affecting family, medical emergency of immediate family member, emergency travel for critically ill relative
- **Approval:** Manager verbal approval; documentation submitted within 5 days of return

## 7. Work-Life Flexibility

### 7.1 Flexible Schedule Arrangements
- **Core Hours:** Employees must be available 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM local time
- **Flex Start/End:** Can start between 7:00 AM - 10:00 AM; end between 3:00 PM - 7:00 PM
- **Compressed Workweek:** 4x10 schedule available with manager approval
- **Part-Time Transition:** Employees may request temporary part-time (60-80%) for family needs (up to 12 months, benefits prorated)

### 7.2 School Involvement Leave
- **Hours:** Up to 16 hours per year for school-related activities
- **Eligible:** Parent-teacher conferences, school events, volunteering at child's school, IEP meetings
- **Notice:** 48-hour advance notice to manager

### 7.3 Family-Friendly Meeting Norms
- No mandatory meetings before 9:00 AM or after 5:00 PM local time
- No-meeting Wednesdays (encouraged but not enforced for client-facing teams)
- Recordings available for all company-wide meetings

## 10. Domestic Partner Benefits

### 10.1 Eligible Relationships
Perficient extends benefits to domestic partners (same-sex or opposite-sex) who:
- Are at least 18 years old
- Share a primary residence for 6+ months
- Are in a committed, exclusive relationship
- Complete a Declaration of Domestic Partnership form with HR

### 10.2 Covered Benefits
Domestic partners and their children receive the same coverage as legal spouses:
- Medical, dental, vision insurance
- Life insurance and AD&D
- FMLA-equivalent leave
- Parental leave (for shared children)
- Bereavement leave
- Relocation assistance

### 10.3 Tax Implications
- Imputed income applies to domestic partner benefits if partner does not qualify as a tax dependent (per IRS guidelines)
- Additional tax cost is reflected on employee's W-2
- Benefits team provides annual cost estimate during Open Enrollment
