# Perficient Recruitment and Hiring Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** March 25, 2024  
**Policy Owner:** VP of Talent Acquisition  
**Applies To:** All hiring managers, interviewers, and Talent Acquisition team members

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy ensures Perficient's hiring practices are fair, consistent, legally compliant, and designed to attract the best talent. A structured approach to recruitment reduces bias, improves quality of hire, and provides a positive candidate experience.

---

## 2. Requisition and Approval

### 2.1 Opening a Position
Before recruiting begins, a job requisition must be approved:
1. Hiring manager submits requisition in Workday with:
   - Job title, level, and reporting structure
   - Business justification
   - Budget confirmation (salary range, sign-on bonus if applicable)
   - Location and remote work eligibility
   - Target start date
2. Requisition approved by: Skip-level manager → Finance → Talent Acquisition

### 2.2 Approval Timelines
- Backfill positions: 3 business days for approval
- Net-new headcount: 5-10 business days (requires VP + Finance)
- Contractor conversions: 3 business days with supporting documentation

### 2.3 Job Descriptions
All job descriptions must:
- Focus on skills and competencies over years of experience
- Include salary range (per pay transparency laws)
- Avoid gendered or exclusionary language
- List only truly required qualifications (vs. "nice to have")
- Include Perficient's EEO statement
- Be reviewed by Talent Acquisition for consistency and compliance

## 3. Sourcing Strategy

### 3.1 Internal Posting
- All positions posted internally for minimum 5 business days
- Internal candidates receive consideration before external candidates
- Internal applicants receive feedback regardless of outcome
- Current employees in good standing may apply after 12 months in role

### 3.2 External Sourcing Channels
- Perficient careers website (careers.perficient.com)
- LinkedIn Recruiter (primary sourcing platform)
- Job boards: Indeed, Glassdoor, Dice (technology roles)
- University partnerships and campus recruiting
- Employee referral program
- Diversity-focused job boards (Jopwell, PowerToFly, Disability:IN)
- Professional communities and meetups
- Recruitment agencies (for specialized or senior roles only, with Procurement approval)

### 3.3 Employee Referral Program
- Bonus: $3,000 (standard roles), $5,000 (senior/hard-to-fill), $7,500 (executive/critical)
- Paid after referred employee completes 90 days
- Not available for recruiting team members or hiring managers for their own roles
- No limit on number of referrals per employee
- Submit referrals through Workday referral portal

## 4. Screening and Selection

### 4.1 Resume Screening
- Talent Acquisition screens within 3 business days of application
- Standardized criteria based on job requirements (not school prestige or company brand)
- AI-assisted screening tools used only as an aid, not sole decision-maker
- All screening criteria documented for compliance purposes

### 4.2 Phone Screen
- 30-minute initial screen by Talent Acquisition recruiter
- Assess: Role alignment, salary expectations, availability, work authorization, cultural fit indicators
- Document standardized notes in Workday
- Advance or decline within 2 business days of screen

### 4.3 Technical Assessment
Role-dependent assessments include:
- **Software Engineering:** Take-home coding exercise (2-4 hour time limit) or live coding session
- **Architecture:** System design whiteboarding
- **Data roles:** SQL/data modeling exercise
- **PM/BA roles:** Case study or process design
- **Management:** Leadership scenario discussion

Assessment guidelines:
- Time limit clearly communicated
- Assessments reviewed blind when possible (remove name/demographics)
- Rubric-based scoring with defined pass criteria
- Accommodations provided upon request

### 4.4 Interview Panel
Standard interview process (adjustable for level):
- 3-4 interviewers for individual contributor roles
- 4-5 interviewers for management roles
- Panel must include at least one person from an underrepresented group
- Each interviewer assesses different competencies (no duplicate coverage)
- Structured questions from approved question bank
- Behavioral ("Tell me about a time...") + situational ("How would you handle...")

### 4.5 Interview Scoring
- Each interviewer submits independent scorecard within 24 hours
- Scoring scale: Strong No (1) → No (2) → Neutral (3) → Yes (4) → Strong Yes (5)
- Debrief meeting within 48 hours of final interview
- Consensus required for hire decisions; single "Strong No" requires discussion
- Hiring manager makes final decision with team input

## 5. Offer Process

### 5.1 Compensation Determination
- Base salary: Within approved range for level and location
- Consider: Candidate experience, internal equity, market data
- Total compensation package presented (base + bonus + equity + benefits)
- Salary ranges reviewed semi-annually against market data (Radford, Mercer)

### 5.2 Offer Approval
- Standard offers within approved range: Hiring manager + Talent Acquisition
- Offers requiring above-range compensation: VP + Compensation team
- Sign-on bonuses over $10,000: VP + Finance approval
- Relocation packages: VP + HR approval

### 5.3 Offer Communication
- Verbal offer by recruiter (phone call preferred)
- Written offer letter within 24 hours of verbal acceptance
- Offer includes: Title, salary, bonus target, start date, benefits summary, contingencies
- Candidate has 5 business days to respond (7 for executive roles)
- Offers are confidential until formally accepted

### 5.4 Background Check
All offers are contingent on successful completion of:
- Criminal history check (7-year lookback)
- Employment verification (last 3 positions)
- Education verification (highest degree)
- Additional checks per role: Credit check (finance roles), drug screening (client-required), security clearance (government clients)

Background check provider: Sterling. Results within 5-7 business days.

## 6. Candidate Experience

### 6.1 Communication Standards
- Acknowledge all applications within 48 hours (automated)
- Update candidates on status within 5 business days of each stage
- Decline candidates promptly with respectful communication
- Provide feedback to final-round candidates who are not selected (upon request)
- Total process target: Initial screen to offer within 3 weeks

### 6.2 Candidate Rights
- Candidates may request accommodations at any stage
- Interview recordings require candidate consent
- Candidate data retained for 2 years per privacy policy
- Candidates may request deletion of their data (unless legally required for compliance)

---

## 7. Compliance

### 7.1 Equal Employment Opportunity
- All hiring decisions based on qualifications and merit
- No discrimination based on protected characteristics
- Reasonable accommodations provided for interviews and assessments
- EEO data collected voluntarily for compliance reporting (not shared with interviewers)

### 7.2 Immigration and Work Authorization
- E-Verify used for all new hires
- I-9 form completed within 3 days of start date
- Visa sponsorship: Available for H-1B and L-1 (with VP and Legal approval)
- PERM/Green Card sponsorship: After 1 year of employment with VP approval

### 7.3 Record Retention
- Applications and interview notes: 2 years
- Offer letters (accepted): Employee file (7 years post-separation)
- Background check results: 2 years
- EEO data: 2 years
- All records stored in compliance with data privacy regulations

## 8. Onboarding Handoff

### 8.1 Pre-Start Preparation
Between offer acceptance and start date:
- IT provisions laptop and accounts (ships 3 days before start)
- Manager creates 30/60/90 day onboarding plan
- Buddy/mentor assigned
- Welcome email with Day 1 logistics sent 1 week before start
- Building access/badge arranged (for office employees)

### 8.2 Day 1 Readiness Checklist
- [ ] Laptop with all required software
- [ ] Email and Teams access active
- [ ] Workday profile created
- [ ] Manager available for first meeting
- [ ] Onboarding schedule shared
- [ ] Benefits enrollment information ready
- [ ] Buddy/mentor introduced
