# Perficient Professional Development and Training Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** February 10, 2024  
**Policy Owner:** VP of Learning & Development  
**Applies To:** All full-time Perficient employees

---

## 1. Philosophy

Perficient invests in continuous learning and professional growth. Technology evolves rapidly, and our competitive advantage depends on our people staying at the forefront of their disciplines. We support multiple pathways to development including formal training, certifications, conferences, mentoring, and hands-on learning.

---

## 2. Training Budget

### 2.1 Individual Training Budget
- **Annual allocation:** $2,500 per employee
- **Budget year:** January 1 - December 31 (does not roll over)
- **Eligible expenses:** Courses, certifications, conferences, books, subscriptions, workshops

### 2.2 Additional Funding
- Manager may approve additional $1,500 from team budget for high-priority development
- VP approval for amounts exceeding $4,000 total
- Certification exam retakes: Covered once (second retake at employee expense)
- Team training: Separate budget managed by L&D team

### 2.3 Requesting Funds
1. Discuss development goals with manager during 1:1 or IDP review
2. Submit training request in Workday (minimum 2 weeks before start)
3. Manager approves or provides alternative suggestion
4. After approval, book through Concur or submit receipt for reimbursement
5. Complete post-training reflection (what was learned, how it applies)

## 3. Certifications

### 3.1 Supported Certifications
Perficient supports certifications relevant to employee roles. Priority certifications by practice:

**Cloud & Infrastructure:**
- AWS Solutions Architect (Associate/Professional)
- Azure Solutions Architect Expert
- Google Cloud Professional Cloud Architect
- Kubernetes (CKA, CKAD)

**Data & Analytics:**
- Databricks Data Engineer Associate/Professional
- Snowflake SnowPro Core/Advanced
- AWS Data Analytics Specialty
- Google Professional Data Engineer

**Development:**
- Salesforce Platform Developer I/II
- Adobe Experience Manager Developer
- Sitecore Developer Certification
- ServiceNow Certified Application Developer

**Project Management:**
- PMP (Project Management Professional)
- PMI-ACP (Agile Certified Practitioner)
- Certified ScrumMaster (CSM)
- SAFe Agilist or SAFe Program Consultant

**Security:**
- CISSP (Certified Information Systems Security Professional)
- CompTIA Security+
- Azure Security Engineer Associate

### 3.2 Certification Benefits
- Exam fee: Fully covered (first attempt, one retake)
- Study materials: Covered within training budget
- Study time: Up to 8 hours of paid study time per certification
- Passing bonus: $500 per certification earned
- Recognition: Published in internal newsletter and LinkedIn endorsement

### 3.3 Certification Maintenance
- Continuing education credits for certification renewals: Covered
- Renewal fees: Covered for active certifications relevant to current role
- Lapsed certifications: Re-examination fee covered once

## 4. Conferences and Events

### 4.1 Attending Conferences
- Budget: Drawn from individual training budget + travel policy
- Approval: Manager approval required; VP approval for international conferences
- Expectations: Share key learnings with team within 2 weeks (presentation or writeup)
- Maximum: 2 conferences per year per employee (exceptions for speakers)

### 4.2 Speaking at Conferences
Employees selected to speak at conferences receive:
- Conference registration and travel covered (separate from training budget)
- Presentation preparation time (up to 16 hours on company time)
- Marketing support for abstract submission and promotion
- Speaking fee retained by employee (if offered by conference)

### 4.3 Perficient-Hosted Events
Perficient hosts internal learning events:
- **Tech Talks:** Bi-weekly, 30-minute lightning talks by employees
- **Innovation Days:** Quarterly hackathon-style events (Friday)
- **Perficient Academy Live:** Monthly deep-dive workshops
- **Leadership Summit:** Annual development conference for managers+

## 5. Mentoring Program

### 5.1 Formal Mentoring
Perficient operates a structured mentoring program:
- **Enrollment:** Open to all employees; sign up during January or July cycles
- **Duration:** 6-month commitment
- **Matching:** L&D team matches mentors and mentees based on goals and expertise
- **Expectations:** Minimum 2 meetings per month (30-60 minutes each)
- **Framework:** Goal-setting, progress tracking, final reflection

### 5.2 Mentor Qualifications
To serve as a mentor:
- Minimum 2 years at Perficient
- Performance rating of "Meets Expectations" or above
- Completion of mentor training module (2 hours)
- Manager approval (mentoring counts as professional development activity)

### 5.3 Reverse Mentoring
Junior employees mentor senior leaders on emerging technologies, social media, or generational perspectives. Pairs are nominated by L&D and VP sponsors.

## 7. Tuition Reimbursement

### 7.1 Eligibility
- Full-time employees with 1+ year of tenure
- Program must be relevant to current role or identified career path
- Accredited institution (university, college, or approved online program)
- Maintained GPA of 3.0 or higher

### 7.2 Reimbursement Limits
- Maximum: $5,250 per calendar year (IRS tax-free limit)
- Additional reimbursement above $5,250 may be available (taxable) with VP approval
- Covers: Tuition, required textbooks, lab fees
- Does not cover: Room and board, application fees, parking

### 7.3 Process
1. Submit pre-approval request to HR (before course registration)
2. HR verifies eligibility and program relevance
3. Employee registers and pays upfront
4. Submit grades and receipts after course completion
5. Reimbursement processed within 30 days of submission

### 7.4 Service Agreement
- Reimbursement over $3,000 requires 12-month service agreement
- If employee voluntarily leaves within 12 months of reimbursement, pro-rated repayment required
- Involuntary separation: No repayment required

## 8. Knowledge Sharing

### 8.1 Expectations
Employees who invest in learning are expected to share knowledge:
- Post-conference summary shared with team
- Blog post on learnings (internal or external)
- Tech Talk presentation (15-30 minutes)
- Updated team wiki or documentation
- Mentoring others on the topic

### 8.2 Recognition
- Knowledge sharing contributions tracked and recognized
- "Expert" badges awarded in the internal directory
- Factored into performance reviews under "Leadership & Collaboration"
- Top contributors featured in quarterly newsletter

## 9. Career Transition Support

### 9.1 Internal Mobility
Perficient supports internal career movement:
- All open positions posted internally for 5 days before external posting
- Employees may apply after 12 months in current role (exceptions with manager approval)
- Internal transfers include onboarding support in new role
- No penalty for exploring internal opportunities

### 9.2 Practice Area Transitions
For employees seeking to change technical practice areas:
- Discuss with current manager and target practice lead
- L&D creates a transition learning plan (typically 3-6 months)
- Shadow assignments on target practice projects
- Structured assessment before formal transfer
- Training budget may be supplemented for transition needs
