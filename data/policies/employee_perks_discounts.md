# Perficient Employee Perks & Discounts

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** April 15, 2024  
**Policy Owner:** Director of Employee Experience  
**Applies To:** All full-time and part-time Perficient employees

---

## 1. Home Office & Remote Work Stipend

### 1.1 One-Time Setup Allowance
New remote employees receive a one-time $1,500 home office setup allowance:
- **Eligible Items:** Desk, ergonomic chair, monitor, keyboard, mouse, webcam, headset, lighting
- **Process:** Purchase items and submit receipts in Workday within first 90 days
- **Reimbursement Timeline:** Processed within 2 pay periods
- **Existing Employees:** Employees transitioning to full-time remote receive $1,000 setup allowance

### 1.2 Monthly Remote Work Stipend
- **Amount:** $75/month for ongoing remote work expenses
- **Eligible:** Internet, electricity, phone plan, office supplies, printer ink/paper
- **Payment:** Added automatically to paycheck (taxable income)
- **Eligibility:** Employees who work remotely 3+ days per week

### 1.3 Equipment Refresh
- **Laptop:** Refreshed every 3 years (standard Perficient-issued hardware)
- **Peripherals:** $300 refresh allowance every 2 years for monitors, keyboards, etc.
- **Ergonomic Assessment:** Free virtual ergonomic consultation available through Benefits team

## 2. Cell Phone & Internet

### 2.1 Cell Phone Reimbursement
- **Monthly Reimbursement:** Up to $75/month for personal cell phone used for business
- **Eligibility:** Employees required to be reachable outside business hours or who use personal phone for client calls
- **Approval:** Manager must confirm business use; one-time approval valid until role changes
- **How to Claim:** Monthly auto-reimbursement once approved (no receipt needed)

### 2.2 Internet Upgrade
- **Purpose:** For employees whose home internet speed is insufficient for video conferencing/cloud tools
- **Allowance:** Up to $50/month above their baseline plan cost
- **Requirement:** Minimum 100 Mbps download speed for remote workers on client-facing projects
- **Approval:** IT department verifies speed requirements

## 3. Commuter Benefits

### 3.1 Pre-Tax Transit Benefits
- **Limit:** Up to $315/month (2024 IRS limit) for public transit passes, vanpool fees
- **Method:** Pre-tax payroll deduction through WageWorks/HealthEquity
- **Eligible:** Subway, bus, commuter rail, ferry, vanpool

### 3.2 Pre-Tax Parking Benefits
- **Limit:** Up to $315/month (2024 IRS limit) for qualified parking
- **Eligible:** Parking at or near work location or at a transit station
- **NOT Eligible:** Residential parking, airport parking (use travel expense policy)

### 3.3 Bicycle Commuter Benefit
- **Amount:** $50/month for employees who bike to work 50%+ of days
- **Eligible Expenses:** Bike maintenance, storage, accessories, bike share memberships
- **Verification:** Self-certification quarterly

## 5. Referral Bonuses

### 5.1 Employee Referral Program
| Role Level | Bonus Amount |
|-----------|-------------|
| Standard hire (IC1-IC3) | $3,000 |
| Senior hire (IC4-IC5) | $4,000 |
| Hard-to-fill roles (tagged by TA) | $5,000 |
| Manager/Director level | $5,000 |
| VP and above | $7,500 |
| Diversity referral bonus (addl.) | +$1,000 |

### 5.2 Payment Schedule
- **50%** paid in first paycheck after referred employee starts
- **50%** paid after referred employee completes 90-day probationary period
- **Tax Treatment:** Taxable bonus income; net payment reflects applicable withholding

### 5.3 Eligibility Rules
- All employees can refer (except Talent Acquisition team members)
- No limit on number of referrals per year
- Referral must be submitted BEFORE the candidate applies
- Referrer cannot be the hiring manager or direct interviewer
- Boomerang employees (returning after 1+ year absence) qualify for referral bonus

### 6.2 Additional Memberships

## 8. Volunteer & Giving Programs

### 8.1 Volunteer Time Off (VTO)
- **Hours:** 16 hours (2 days) of paid VTO per year
- **Eligible Activities:** Volunteering with registered 501(c)(3) nonprofits
- **Team Events:** Department-organized volunteer days (additional to individual VTO)
- **Approval:** Submit VTO request in Workday (manager approval within 2 days)

### 8.2 Charitable Match
- **Match Ratio:** 1:1 dollar-for-dollar matching
- **Annual Cap:** $1,000 per employee per year
- **Eligible Organizations:** IRS-recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofits (excluding political, religious worship)
- **How to Submit:** Upload donation receipt in Workday; matched amount donated within 30 days

## 9. Relocation Assistance

### 9.1 Standard Relocation Package
For employees relocating 50+ miles for a Perficient role:
- **Lump Sum:** $5,000 for individual; $10,000 for employee with family
- **Moving Expenses:** Reimbursement up to $8,000 (receipts required)
- **Temporary Housing:** Up to 30 days of corporate housing or hotel
- **House-Hunting Trip:** 1 paid trip (flights + 3 nights hotel + car rental)

### 9.2 Service Commitment
- Relocation assistance requires a 2-year service commitment
- Voluntary departure within 1 year: Repay 100% of relocation costs
- Voluntary departure in year 2: Repay 50% of relocation costs
- Involuntary termination: No repayment required
